# Recruiter Copilot

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

Recruiter Copilot is an AI-powered Recruitment Operating System designed for recruitment agencies and internal HR teams.

The product is NOT an AI chatbot.

The product is NOT another ATS.

The product is NOT another CRM.

The product is an AI-first Recruitment Workspace where every recruiter works together with an AI Copilot.

The AI acts as a professional recruitment assistant that helps recruiters complete repetitive tasks, organize candidate knowledge, search candidates intelligently, generate documents, summarize information, and continuously learn from recruitment history.

The AI never replaces the recruiter.

The recruiter always makes the final decision.

The AI only assists.

---

# Core Philosophy

Everything in the system revolves around Candidate Knowledge.

Every candidate entering the system becomes structured knowledge.

A PDF is not just a file.

A LinkedIn profile is not just a webpage.

A Resume is not just a document.

Everything becomes structured data.

The system continuously transforms unstructured recruitment information into searchable knowledge.

---

# Product Goals

Reduce manual recruitment work by at least 70%.

Reduce CV reading time by at least 90%.

Allow recruiters to find suitable candidates within seconds.

Remember everything that recruiters usually forget.

Provide intelligent recommendations during every recruitment stage.

Centralize candidate knowledge.

Standardize recruitment workflows.

Become the operating system for recruitment companies.

---

# What AI Should Do

AI SHOULD

Parse resumes.

Understand job descriptions.

Match candidates.

Generate candidate summaries.

Generate client-ready candidate profiles.

Recommend candidates.

Detect duplicate candidates.

Generate interview questions.

Summarize phone calls.

Summarize interview notes.

Generate follow-up messages.

Generate Boolean search.

Recommend recruiters' next actions.

Answer questions using company recruitment data.

Continuously improve candidate knowledge.

---

# What AI Should NEVER Do

AI never makes hiring decisions.

AI never sends emails automatically without recruiter confirmation.

AI never rejects candidates automatically.

AI never modifies candidate information silently.

AI never deletes recruitment history.

AI never changes business data without explicit user approval.

---

# Product Principles

AI is always explainable.

Every recommendation must include reasoning.

Every AI output is editable.

Nothing generated by AI is permanent until saved.

Every important action is auditable.

Every business object has history.

Every change has a reason.

No hidden automation.

Recruiters must always stay in control.
